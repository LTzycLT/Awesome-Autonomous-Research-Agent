# Awesome Autonomous Research Agent

A curated list of papers and projects on autonomous AI systems that conduct scientific research.

**Scope**: Systems that autonomously perform research tasks — including hypothesis generation, experiment design/execution, result analysis, and paper writing — with minimal human intervention. Execution frameworks widely used as research scaffolds are also included.

**Format rule**: All descriptions are taken verbatim or paraphrased directly from the paper abstract or project README. No editorial judgment. Entries are sorted by release date (descending).
